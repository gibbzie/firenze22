UN GIORNO FUORI DI FIRENZE

1. Regional Bus

Walk to this station and catch the 131 for 34 stops (~1hour, 35 minutes).

2. Get Off at San Domenico

Get off the bus at this station and walk to the Torture Museum.

3. Bus Back to Firenze

The last bus is at ~20:00. NO WORRIES

or 1. FlixBus

It seems that it is slightly quicker (possibly cheaper, definitely easier to look up the price and book) if we take a FlixBus to and from Siena.

~0:50 non-stop. ~€7-12 each way, per person.

Rocca di Castagnoli [Agriturismo]

We have to catch a bus FROM Siena. We can stop in Siena first, see everything, go to the Torture Museum, and then jump on a bus to the Agriturismo.

Last tasting starts at ~17:00

There is a bus TO here but none BACK. The taxi back is ~€70 in total (waiting for confirmation).
